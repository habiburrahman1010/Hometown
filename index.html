Welcome to Munshiganj


Munshiganj, also known as Bikrampur in ancient times, is a historic district in Bangladesh. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, ancient ruins, and beautiful rivers. The region has played a significant role in shaping the country's history, producing many notable scholars and historical figures.

Historical Places:
  1. Idrakpur Fort
  2. Baba Adam Mosque
  3. Sonarang Twin Temples
  4. Rampal Dighi
  5. Ballal Sen's Palace
 Popular Foods:
  • Hilsha Fish Curry
  • Murali Sweet
  • Pitha (Rice Cakes)
  • Local Date Molasses
  • Chingri Malai Curry
